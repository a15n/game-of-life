The universe of the Game of Life is an infinite 2D orthogonal grid of cells, each of which is either alive or dead.

During an evolution each cell interacts with its eight neighbors and one of the following transitions may occur:

  • Under Population: A live cell with <2 live neighbors dies
  • Equilibrium: A live cell with 2 - 3 live neighbors survives
  • Overcrowding: A live cell with >three neighbors dies
  • Reproduction: A dead cell with 3 live neighbors becomes a live cell
